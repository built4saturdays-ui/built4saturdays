Our Story

We are former Division 1 teammates from Bryant University who lived the grind together. We founded Built For Saturdays to bridge the gap between locker room reality and NIL opportunity. By combining a record-breaking All-American career with deep strategic experience, we provide athletes the blueprint to build, monetize, and protect their brands from the college level to the pros.

Marc Taglieri

Co-Founder | 2024 Graduate

Marc brings a high football IQ and a reputation for disciplined execution to the NIL space. A four-year letter winner at Tight End for Bryant University, Marc appeared in nearly 40 games, recording career touchdowns against major opponents like Delaware, Richmond, and Akron.

  • D1 Veteran: 39+ games played at the Division 1 level.
  • Versatile Playmaker: Career TDs against FBS and top FCS programs.
  • Academic Leader: Multiple NEC Academic Honor Roll selections.

Landon Ruggieri

Co-Founder | All-American WR | 2024 Graduate

Landon is one of the most prolific wide receivers in Bryant University history. A CAA First-Team selection and All-American, he led the nation in receiving touchdowns (15) in 2024. Landon’s career was defined by record-breaking production, finishing second all-time in program history.

  • Nation-Leader: #1 in D1 (FCS) receiving touchdowns in 2024.
  • Record Setter: Set the DI program record for single-season receptions (69).
  • Elite Honors: All-American, 2x All-New England, and First-Team All-CAA.
